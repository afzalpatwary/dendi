Whatever It Takes.

Labs are one the most important, yet underserved components of the healthcare ecosystem. In this increasingly complex, competitive, and constrained environment, the importance of high-quality solutions partners is paramount, and that’s why we’re here: to redefine the standard for lab software.

However, our mission means more to us than being yet another software vendor. We exist because we’re “all-in” on the success of our partner labs. This means going beyond having a great product, solution, and team. This means that we’re not afraid of doing the dirty work, filling in the gaps where needed, and even doing things that defy conventional logic – whatever it takes.
